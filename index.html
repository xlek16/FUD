Features

Built around what actually matters

Every feature on this platform exists because solo queue is frustrating. We fix the specific things that make it bad.

Smart Matching Algorithm

We don't just show you random players. Our matching considers rank range, region, play hours, preferred roles, and playstyle compatibility — so every suggestion actually makes sense for you.

Verified Ranks, No Catfishing

Link your Riot, Steam, or Epic account and your rank is pulled automatically. Anyone who lies about being Radiant gets caught immediately. What you see is what you get.

In-app Chat

Talk before you queue. No need to share socials with strangers until you know you want to.

Gameplay Clips

Pin your best highlights to your profile. Let your clutches and aces do the talking for you.

Multi-game Profiles

One profile, all your games. Switch between Valorant and CS2 without creating separate accounts.

Duo Reviews

Rate teammates after sessions. Build your reputation, find trusted players, avoid toxic ones.

Session Scheduling

Set your play window. Find players who are actually online when you want to grind.

Squad Builder

Need a full team, not just a duo? Use our team finder to build a complete 3-5 player squad.
